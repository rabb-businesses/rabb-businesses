Our Story

Founded in 2015, Rabb Businesses began as a small consultancy firm with a big vision - to transform the business landscape in the UAE through innovative solutions and expert guidance.

Our founder, Mr. Ahmed Rabb, recognized the growing need for comprehensive business support services in the region's rapidly expanding economy. What started as a one-person operation has now grown into a team of over 50 dedicated professionals serving clients across all seven emirates.

Over the years, we've helped more than 200 businesses - from startups to established enterprises - navigate the complexities of the UAE market. Our hands-on approach and deep understanding of local business regulations have made us a preferred partner for companies looking to establish or expand their presence in the region.

In 2020, we expanded our services to include e-commerce consultancy, recognizing the digital transformation sweeping across the business world. This forward-thinking approach has allowed our clients to stay ahead of market trends and technological advancements.

Today, Rabb Businesses stands as a testament to the power of perseverance, innovation, and client-focused service. Our journey continues as we explore new ways to empower businesses and contribute to the UAE's dynamic economy.
